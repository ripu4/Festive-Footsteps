About Us

Discover Who We Are - Your Festival Guides

Welcome to Festive Footsteps! I am Ripudaman, and this website was inspired by my cousin Vikas Tyagi, who faced challenges celebrating Indian festivals while living abroad.

Festive Footsteps aims to bridge the gap between tradition and distance, providing comprehensive guides on the cultural significance, rituals, and best practices for celebrating Indian festivals.

Here, you’ll find step-by-step instructions, traditional recipes, and creative ideas to help you reconnect with your roots and enjoy these vibrant celebrations, no matter where you are.

Join me on this journey to embrace our heritage, create lasting memories, and celebrate the spirit of togetherness!
